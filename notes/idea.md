# Overview
An application, that talks to you real time as you play videogames (or any other activity), and has context of the stuff that is happening on your screen.

Sluggish plan:
- An MCP server, that clicks a screenshot of your screen every 10 seconds, and sends a 30 word description of what is happening on your screen. 

- The voice to voice model, gets this context and talks about it to the user.

I wanted all of it done locally (possibly using hella system resources) but the goal is to make it all offline. 

Semantic Overview:
A companion that can accompany people as they play games alone, and act as a buddy.
